About ExpenseTracker

Track Your Expenses Easily

ExpenseTracker is a simple yet powerful tool to help you manage your personal finances. Keep track of your daily expenses, analyze spending patterns, and make informed financial decisions.

Key Features:
  • Easy expense tracking with categories
  • Visual charts and statistics
  • Monthly and weekly expense analysis
  • Category-wise expense distribution
  • Secure user authentication
How to Use:
  1. Create an account or login
  2. Add your daily expenses
  3. Categorize your spending
  4. View detailed analytics
  5. Make better financial decisions
